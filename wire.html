About Me

Alright! Since you are so curious, allow me to tell you a little bit about the person behind the mask... I mean computer screen. Whenever I am not on the computer trying to find different languages to learn and help my friends or family, then I am either at my church volunteering my time, playing a massive video game with a huge group of people (I am one of the vice leaders, which I am still regretting to this day), being a 'helpful ear' to the LGBTQ+ community or staring at the market trying to find a good stock/option to pick up.
However, most of the time I will be on the computer trying to keep up with the ever evolving web and all of the content and information that it has procured over the lifespan of its existence. Memes? Trending topics? Anime? Games? Movies? Dark web (wait...) I have access to it all!
As for working with others? The more, the merrier! A collective group will always finish a project faster than someone doing one by themselves. Also, having different brains and pair of eyes will always give an alternative look or angle of how to go about solving different things!
